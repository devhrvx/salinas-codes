UV Protection

Zinc Oxide and Titanium Dioxide:

These mineral-based ingredients work together to block both UVA and UVB rays, providing broad-spectrum protection by reflecting harmful sunlight off the skin.

Cooling Effect

Menthol and Aloe Vera:

Together, these ingredients provide a cooling and soothing effect. Menthol activates cold-sensitive receptors in the skin for an immediate cooling sensation, while Aloe Vera, known for its calming properties, enhances this effect and can offer additional relief, especially when the sunscreen is refrigerated before use.

Antimicrobial

Ethyl Alcohol:

Included for its antimicrobial properties, ethyl alcohol helps eliminate bacteria on the skin, keeping it clean and refreshed with every application of our sunscreen spray.

Whitening and Anti-aging

Retinal (Retinaldehyde), Vitamin C, and Niacinamide:

Provides powerful benefits in skincare. Retinal, a potent derivative of Vitamin A, offers whitening and anti-aging effects while being gentler on the skin. Vitamin C, as an antioxidant, neutralizes free radicals, boosts collagen, and helps reduce wrinkles and sagging. Niacinamide calms the skin with its anti-inflammatory properties, protects against environmental damage, and enhances elasticity by promoting collagen production. Together, they work to promote a youthful, firm, and radiant complexion.

How to use:

  1. Shake well before use.
  2. Spray generously on skin.
  3. Reapply as needed, especially after swimming, sweating, or towel drying.
  4. For best results, apply 15 minutes before sun exposure.
  5. Store the product properly in a cool, dry, and away from direct sunligght.

    Precautions

  • Avoid contact with eyes. If contact occurs, rinse thoroughly with water.
  • Do not apply to broken or irritated skin.
  • Keep out of reach of children.
  • If irritation or rash occurs, discontinue use and consult a doctor.
  • For external use only.
  • Store in a cool, dry place, and avoid direct sunlight.
  • Flammable.
