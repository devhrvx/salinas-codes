UV Protection
Zinc Oxide and Titanium Dioxide: These mineral-based ingredients work together to block both UVA and UVB rays, providing broad-spectrum protection by reflecting harmful sunlight off the skin.
Cooling Effect
Menthol and Aloe Vera: Together, these ingredients provide a cooling and soothing effect. Menthol activates cold-sensitive receptors in the skin for an immediate cooling sensation, while Aloe Vera, known for its calming properties, enhances this effect and can offer additional relief, especially when the sunscreen is refrigerated before use.
Antimicrobial
Ethyl Alcohol: Included for its antimicrobial properties, ethyl alcohol helps eliminate bacteria on the skin, keeping it clean and refreshed with every application of our sunscreen spray.
Whitening and Anti-aging
Retinal (Retinaldehyde), Vitamin C, and Niacinamide: Provides powerful benefits in skincare. Retinal, a potent derivative of Vitamin A, offers whitening and anti-aging effects while being gentler on the skin. Vitamin C, as an antioxidant, neutralizes free radicals, boosts collagen, and helps reduce wrinkles and sagging. Niacinamide calms the skin with its anti-inflammatory properties, protects against environmental damage, and enhances elasticity by promoting collagen production. Together, they work to promote a youthful, firm, and radiant complexion.
